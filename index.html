Train of Consequences

Summary

This static site will help people learn about “Train of Consequences”, a song by the metal band Megadeth.

Description

There is a capture-the-flag type of scenario. We have a train shown below that moves from left (initial point) to right (finish line). Each correct answer to the question will move the train closer to the finish line. You have to answer four questions one by one.

Question #1: What year was Train of Consequences released?

Answer: 1973 1988 1994 1999
